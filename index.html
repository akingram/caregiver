What We Offer

Comprehensive Care Services

Our well-trained care support staff are committed to delivering standard support services in accordance with international practice and standards.

Elderly Care & Support

Short-term and long-term support for elderly parents in need due to disabilities or old age, including daily living assistance and companionship.

Post-Hospital Care

Professional support for hospital discharged patients needing post-hospital care to recover safely and comfortably at home.

Pediatric Care

Nurturing support for children living with disabilities, including autism and other complex needs, delivered by trained pediatric care specialists.

Daily Living Support

Comprehensive assistance with daily routines and activities for persons living with disabilities, promoting independence and dignity.

Respite Care Services

Short-term relief for primary caregivers, ensuring your loved one continues to receive quality care while you take a well-deserved break.

Excursions & Companionship

Safe, engaging social outings and companionship that enrich the lives of our service users and reduce social isolation.

Our Story

Care Built on Compassion and Trust

Alice Care Homes Limited is a compassionate care service provider with professional carers dedicated to supporting and improving the quality of life for challenged individuals and persons living with disabilities.

We provide personalized care solutions designed to meet the physical, emotional, and social needs of our service users in a safe, comfortable, and supportive environment in accordance with international practice and standard. Regulated by the Ministry of Health of Nigeria.
