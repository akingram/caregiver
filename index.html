Our Story

Redefining Caregiving With Heart & Excellence

Care Analytics was born from a simple observation: families deserve better when seeking care for their loved ones.

Founded in 2020 by a team of healthcare professionals and technologists, we've built a platform that combines rigorous caregiver screening with personalized matching. We believe that exceptional care happens when skills meet compassion, and when caregivers truly understand the unique needs of each family.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of families across the country, providing peace of mind through quality care.
