Our Story

Care Built on Compassion and Trust

Care Analytics was founded in 2018 by a team of healthcare professionals and family caregivers who experienced firsthand how difficult finding quality, reliable care could be during life's most vulnerable moments.

What began as a small network of vetted caregivers has grown into a trusted care platform serving thousands of families — yet we remain deeply personal. Every request is reviewed by a human being. Every match is made with care.

Quality Assurance

How We Vet Every Caregiver

Only 1 in 8 applicants makes it through our rigorous vetting process. Your family deserves nothing less.

1

Enhanced Background Checks

Every caregiver undergoes a comprehensive DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) check, including enhanced criminal record disclosures and reference verification from at least three previous employers.

2

Qualification Verification

We independently verify all stated qualifications — nursing certificates, first aid training, dementia care certifications — with original documentation and awarding bodies.

3

In-Depth Interview & Assessment

A structured interview with one of our senior care coordinators, including scenario-based assessments, soft skill evaluation, and cultural values alignment testing.

4

Supervised Trial Period

All newly approved caregivers complete a supervised placement period before being matched independently, with ongoing performance reviews and family feedback integration.

5

Continuous Professional Development

Mandatory annual retraining in areas including safeguarding, first aid, infection control, and care-specific upskilling keeps our team at the forefront of best practice.

6

Ongoing Quality Reviews

Regular welfare checks, anonymous family feedback surveys, and periodic re-vetting ensure our standards never slip — for new or long-standing team members.
